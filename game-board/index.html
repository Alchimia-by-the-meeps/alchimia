Instructions for playing Alchimia

  • Place the drawn, face up, tile onto the game board by clicking on the grid square where you would like to place the tile
  • When you mouse over the square, you will see a hover preview of your tile placement
  • You can only place the tile once, so be sure about your placement before you click
  • You must place the tile in such a way that it continues the landscape and the illustration
  • After you have placed the last tile the game will end and you will be able to see your generated Kingdom
